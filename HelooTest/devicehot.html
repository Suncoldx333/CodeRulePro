If you are using our 4th generation of devices and set the device to AP configuration mode, you will see the AP name in system Wi-Fi settings shown as “[DeviceName]_xxx” (xxx is the last 3 characters of MAC address). For example:

● Smart plugs: “Smart Plug_123”

● Universal Remote: “Universal Remote_456”

● Smart Bulb: “Smart Bulb_987”

You may also find the AP name for some devices (1st-3rd gen products) shown as “BroadLinkProv” if none of the above names are listed.

If you still cannot find a matched AP name of your device, please contact our customer service: service@brightvillehk.com
